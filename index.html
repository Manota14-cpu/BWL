FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does a typical project take? +

A landing page takes 1–2 weeks, a web app 4–8 weeks, and an enterprise solution 8–16 weeks depending on scope. We provide a precise timeline during the discovery phase.

What's included in the price? +

Design, development, testing, deployment, and post-launch support are always included. Pro and Enterprise packages also include maintenance, documentation, and a handoff session.

What technologies do you use? +

We choose the best stack for each project — typically React, Next.js, Node.js, TypeScript, and Python. For AI projects we leverage OpenAI, Anthropic, and custom fine-tuned models.

How does payment work? +

We bill in milestones: 30% to start, 40% on design approval, 30% on delivery. We accept wire transfers, PayPal, and USDC.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance? +

Absolutely. We offer monthly retainer packages that include updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and priority support.

How do we get started? +

Book a free 30-minute call. We'll discuss your idea, answer your questions, and send you a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing within 48 hours.
